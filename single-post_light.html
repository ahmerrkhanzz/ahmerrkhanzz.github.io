Hi, i am Blake Dexter. I am a 25 years old Freelance Creative Director born, raised and living in New York City, USA. I love Design since i was a small kid. Especially minimalistic and industrial design became my favourites. So i decide to make my love to my job. After University i worked some years for several agency to empower my skills.

To get a impression for what i am standing for you will find some outstanding design works on Dribbble and Behance. Follow me on Twitter and Instagram to get daily news or see my full job portfolio on Linkedin. For project requests please send me an email or use the contact form at the bottom.
